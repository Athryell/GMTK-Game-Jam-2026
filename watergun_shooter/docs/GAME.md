# Watergun shooter

## Concept
You're in the desert, you have a water gun, a water shotgun, and some zombies
are trying to kill you. You move around and shoot with the weapons in order to 
kill the zombies.

# Gameplay
You have the following bars:
1. water stamina: your total water amount to use
2. moving energy: amount of water in your body, you consume this by moving
3. water gun ammo: amount of water in the water gun, you consume this by shooting
4. water shotgun ammo: amount of water in the shotgun, you consume this by shooting

The moving energy, water gun ammo and water shotgun ammo also consume with passing of time
(it's a desert, water evaporates). 

When you kill zombies, they drop water that you can collect and that refill your water stamina.
Then, you have to decide where to allocate the water you collect:
- drink, fill up moving energy
- refill water gun
- refill water shotgun

When moving energy finishes, you move very slowly. 
When gun/shotgun amm finishes, you can't shoot.

Game is basically a survive and decide how to allocate the water you collect. The countdown
element is in the fact that the water evaporates so you are also pressured in moving and shooting
to collect more.

Of course, the shotgun is stronger than the gun but consumes water faster and viceversa.
